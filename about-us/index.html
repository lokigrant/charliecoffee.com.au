About

 

 

Why stop at one cup of coffee? Why not enjoy the experience further?

At Charlie Coffee, we believe in flavour. We love coffee and look forward to the second and third cup. Our mouths don’t like harsh, bitter coffee that dehydrates you. SO, we created the Charlie Coffee blend.

We’ve brought together the flowery, caramel-based soul of South America and the spicy heart of Northern Africa in a marriage of taste.

We roast the blend just enough to give it the strength needed to carry this flavour in milk and the body needed to give you that wake-up call you look forward to each morning.

We don’t make the mistake of roasting too much, however. Importantly, the Charlie Coffee blend can be drunk black and not tear your guts out… the way our Master Roaster likes it!

We like Charlie Coffee morning, noon or night.
